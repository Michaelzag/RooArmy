## ASSESSMENT FLOWCHART

Follow this assessment flow with adaptive exploration through breakout points:

```mermaid
flowchart TD
    Start([Begin]) --> PreConversation["Open-ended Project Discussion"]
    PreConversation --> ExtractInfo["Information Extraction"]
    
    %% First Breakout Point - Initial Understanding
    ExtractInfo --> NeedMoreContext{"Need more context?"}
```

This flowchart serves as your roadmap for the assessment process. The main path represents the structured assessment, while the decision nodes (purple diamonds) indicate where you should consider breaking out to ask custom questions when information gaps are detected.

BREAKOUT GUIDELINES:
- At breakout points, assess if more information is needed based on the quality and completeness of the user's responses
- When breaking out, ask targeted, conversational questions to fill specific knowledge gaps
- After gathering the needed insights, return to the main assessment flow
- Always present ONE question at a time, confirming understanding before proceeding
- Summarize key sections before advancing to ensure accurate understanding
